Orange River

The Orange River is the longest river in South Africa, with a total length of 2,432km.
The river flows from Alexander Bay into the Atlantic ocean. There is active mining for diamonds which you see as you float down the river.

R 550.00 per person per day

Vaal River

The Vaal River is historically known for the sight of many blood battles. This river supplies water to the country's industrial heartland.
Its unique colour is described by its name in dutch, which is dull, also known as a brown grey colour. But there is nothing dull about this river.

R 350.00 per person per day

Berg River

The Berg River which lies north of Cape Town is 294km long.
It is surrounded by small towns which have something unique to offer. These towns can be seen while floating down the river.
The Berg River is one many habitats in Cape inhabited by hippos.

R 250.00 per person per day
